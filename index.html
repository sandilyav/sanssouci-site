Welcome to Sans Souci

Just 30 mins from Chennai Port, past Royapuram in Ennore, is “Sans Souci” – French for “Without Worry”. A tranquil spot with a checkered history of being the location for more than 100 South Indian films. Right on the Ennore Creek with direct access to the water.

History

Sans Souci comes with a chequered history starting in 1906 when it was part of the property called Salt Bungalow. The Salt Bungalow was built as the office of the Salt Commissioner during British rule in India. The property was acquired by the current owners in 1962 and has served as a scenic picnic location for the family bringing many years of family joy and memories. It also has been the site of more than 100 film shootings with the greats of Tamil Cinema – Kamal Haasan and Mohan shooting in Salt Bungalow and Sans Souci. In the late 2000s, a portion of the property was acquired for the construction of the Ennore expressway connector bridge to Kattupalli island – the site of the Kamaraj Port. So the property has since been divided into two – The original Salt Bungalow and the new Sans Souci.

Activities

Camping

Experience nature at its best with camping at Sans Souci. Wake up to the sound of the Ennore Creek, explore the area, and end the day cuddled in a cozy tent. Sans Souci offers a range of activities for all levels of campers.

Kayaking

Supported by the Royal Madras Yatch Club, Sans Souci offers boating with kayaking, paddle boarding and even motor boating around the Ennore Creek

Angling

Regarded as one of Chennais premier angling spots by the vibrant Chennai Angling community, come on over and go fishing in the beautiful Ennore backwaters.

Parties

Hosting a party? What better way than on the water front. Take guests on a catamaran ride and party the night away overlooking the backwaters. Rave anyone?
