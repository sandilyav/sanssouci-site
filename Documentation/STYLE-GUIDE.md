
Sans Souci Style Guide

Visual Identity System v1.0

Location Context: Ennore Creek
Primary Audience: Chennai Gen Z / Young Professionals

⸻

1. Brand Personality

Core Traits
	•	Cinematic
	•	Minimal
	•	Calm but confident
	•	Youthful without trying too hard
	•	Nature-led
	•	Authentic

Avoid
	•	Overly tropical colors
	•	Neon gradients
	•	Overly playful fonts
	•	Overuse of emojis
	•	“Tourist brochure” look

⸻

2. Color System

The palette must reflect:
	•	Water
	•	Golden hour
	•	Sand
	•	Night bonfire glow

Primary Palette

Name	Usage	HEX
Deep Creek	Primary background	#0F1E24
Coastal Slate	Section background alt	#1F2F36
Mist Grey	Body background	#F4F6F7
Sand	Accent background	#E8DCC8
Sunset Gold	Accent / CTA hover	#F5B841
Flame	CTA primary	#E65C2F

Usage Rules
	•	Dark sections for hero and immersive sections
	•	Light sections for readability
	•	Accent color used sparingly
	•	No more than 2 accent colors per page

⸻

3. Typography

Must feel modern and editorial.

Headline Font

Primary:
Playfair Display or DM Serif Display
(For cinematic elegance)

Usage:
	•	Hero headings
	•	Section headers
	•	Large pull quotes

⸻

Body Font

Primary:
Inter or Manrope

Usage:
	•	Body text
	•	Buttons
	•	Navigation
	•	Metadata

⸻

Typography Rules
	•	Hero H1: 36–48px mobile
	•	Section Headings: 24–28px
	•	Body text: 16–18px
	•	Tight line-height for headlines
	•	Generous line-height for body text
	•	No all caps paragraphs

⸻

4. Imagery Guidelines

The imagery is the hero.

Photography Style

Must feel:
	•	Natural light
	•	Minimal editing
	•	Slightly warm tones
	•	Real people
	•	Movement captured mid-action
	•	Water reflections emphasized

Avoid:
	•	Stock-photo look
	•	Over-saturated HDR
	•	Fake sky replacements
	•	Staged smiling group shots

⸻

Image Composition Rules
	•	70% of images must include water
	•	Prioritize golden hour
	•	Use silhouettes often
	•	Include wide negative space
	•	Avoid cluttered frames

⸻

5. Layout System

Design Philosophy

Airy. Spacious. Swipeable.

Mobile-first vertical rhythm.

⸻

Section Spacing
	•	80px desktop vertical padding
	•	56px mobile vertical padding
	•	Never stack heavy content blocks without breathing room

⸻

Card Design
	•	16px border radius
	•	Subtle shadow only on hover
	•	Large thumbnail images
	•	Bold short headlines
	•	Minimal text

⸻

Buttons

Primary:
	•	Solid Flame (#E65C2F)
	•	White text
	•	8px–12px padding
	•	Rounded 8px

Secondary:
	•	Outline style
	•	1px stroke
	•	Transparent background

No gradients.
No drop shadows.

⸻

6. Iconography

Use thin-line icons.

Style:
	•	Minimal
	•	Monoline
	•	Rounded edges

Icons needed:
	•	Tide
	•	Wind
	•	Sunset
	•	Kayak
	•	Camera
	•	Bonfire

Avoid:
	•	Cartoon icons
	•	Emoji-only navigation

⸻

7. Motion & Interaction

Subtle motion only.

Allowed
	•	Fade in sections
	•	Parallax water effect (very subtle)
	•	Hover image zoom (1.03 scale)
	•	Smooth scroll

Not Allowed
	•	Bounce animations
	•	Loud transitions
	•	Auto-play audio
	•	Flashing elements

⸻

8. Instagram Integration Design

Reels grid must:
	•	Use 9:16 preview ratio
	•	Rounded corners
	•	Minimal overlay text
	•	Soft dark overlay for readability

Tagline example overlay:

Shot at Sans Souci.

⸻

9. “Today at Sans Souci” Widget Styling

Must feel like a modern marine dashboard.
	•	Dark background
	•	Soft glowing accent
	•	Minimal line separators
	•	Clean grid layout

Example styling:

Tide: Rising
Wind: 8 km/h
Golden Hour: 5:42 PM
Reflection Rating: 8/10

Use soft gold highlight for golden hour.

⸻

10. Copy Tone Guide

Voice:

Confident. Minimal. Slightly playful.

Examples:

Instead of:

“Enjoy scenic waterfront views.”

Say:

“This sunset does half the editing for you.”

Instead of:

“We offer kayaking.”

Say:

“Float into golden hour.”

⸻

11. Creator Layer Design

For the “Creators” page:
	•	Dark mode dominant
	•	Cinematic visuals
	•	Clean grid gallery
	•	Minimal copy
	•	High impact visuals

Tagline idea:

Bring a camera. We’ll handle the light.

⸻

12. SEO Page Style Variation

For long-form SEO pages:
	•	Light background
	•	More structured typography
	•	Structured headings
	•	Pull quotes
	•	Embedded visuals
	•	Minimal decorative elements

Keep editorial feel.

⸻

13. Brand Tagline Options
	•	Creekside. Cinematic.
	•	Where Your Weekend Looks Better.
	•	Chennai’s Golden Hour Spot.
	•	Adventure, But Make It Aesthetic.

⸻

14. Overall Visual North Star

If someone screenshots your homepage:

It should feel like:
	•	A premium outdoor journal
	•	A hidden European creek escape
	•	A modern adventure brand

Not:
	•	A resort website
	•	A boating club
	•	A tourist operator
